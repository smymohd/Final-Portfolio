About Me

My name is Sumaya Yusuf, I'm Somalian and I moved from Kuwait seven years ago to join my family. I have nursing diploma and I'm a full time student at Metropolitan State University. My goal is to publish my art works of prints and textile and to create websites that will provide different kinds of services to the Somalian communities in Minnesota. I'm very proud of myself to be able to finish a coding bootcamp course when I had no experience in any related subject.
